About Us

Farm History

Located in the heart of Mthatha Eastern Cape, Rural Roots Farm has been a cornerstone of agricultural tradition since 2007. Originally founded by Babini Sitshoni, our farm has evolved over generations, blending modern innovation with time-honored farming practices.

Mission Statement

At Rural Roots, our mission is simple yet profound: to cultivate the land with care, respect natureâ€™s rhythms, and provide our community with wholesome, sustainably grown produce. We are dedicated to promoting environmental stewardship and supporting local food systems.

Philosophy

We believe in the power of sustainable agriculture to not only nourish bodies but also to enrich communities and preserve our natural resources. Our farming practices prioritize soil health, biodiversity, and animal welfare, ensuring that every harvest reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Community Involvement

As proud members of the Mthatha community, we actively participate in local farmers' markets, collaborate with nearby schools to educate future generations about farming, and contribute surplus produce to local food banks.

Meet the Founder

Babini Sitshoni, founder of Rural Roots Farm, leads our passionate team of farmers and caretakers. Together, we bring a wealth of knowledge and dedication to every aspect of farm life, from planting seeds to harvesting crops.

Why Choose Us?

  • Freshness Guaranteed: Our produce is picked at peak ripeness, ensuring exceptional flavor and nutrition.
  • Support Local: By choosing Rural Roots Farm, you're supporting a local business committed to sustainability and community.
  • Transparency: We believe in transparency and invite you to visit our farm and see firsthand how we grow your food.

Our Products

Our pasture-raised sheep are known for their tender meat and rich flavor. Raised with care and allowed to graze freely, our sheep produce high-quality wool and delicious meat products.

Our cows roam freely on lush pastures, producing milk that is used to create artisanal dairy products such as cheese and butter. We also offer grass-fed beef known for its superior taste and nutritional benefits.

We grow a variety of seasonal vegetables using sustainable farming practices. From crispy lettuce to vibrant tomatoes, our vegetables are harvested at peak freshness to bring the flavors of the farm directly to your table.

Enjoy the sweetness of nature with our selection of fresh fruits. From juicy apples to succulent berries, each fruit is hand-picked to ensure you experience the true taste of our orchards.
